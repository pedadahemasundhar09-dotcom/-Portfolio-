Frequently Asked

Answers before you ask.

Still have questions? Book a free 30-minute call — no pitch, just answers.

What's included in each package?

Starter (₹7,999) covers up to 3 pages and basic SEO. Growth (₹14,999) — our most popular tier — adds premium UI/UX, WhatsApp & Maps, and a blog. Premium (₹22,999) adds CMS, custom UI/UX and advanced animations. Add-ons like extra pages, logos and SEO are available on request.

How fast do you deliver?

Starter ships in 3–5 days, Growth in 5–7 days, Premium in 7–10 days. Discovery, design and development run in parallel with direct founder attention — no juniors, no hand-offs.

Who owns the website?

You do — 100%. Code, design files, domain, hosting and content are all transferred to you at delivery. No lock-in, ever.

What if I'm not happy with the result?

If we miss the promised quality bar, performance score or ship date, we keep working for free until we hit it — zero exceptions. Every package also includes revisions.

How do payments work?

50% to kick off, 50% on delivery. Larger custom projects can be split across milestones. We accept UPI, bank transfer, and all major cards.

Is SEO included, and will my site be fast?

Every package includes SEO — basic on Starter, advanced on Growth, and premium technical SEO on Premium. We target 95+ Lighthouse scores and design mobile-first on every build.

Do you handle hosting, domains and migrations?

Yes — hosting setup from ₹1,000, domain setup from ₹500. We can also migrate your old WordPress, Wix or Squarespace site with 301 redirects to protect your SEO.

Do you sign NDAs or work with clients outside India?

We'll sign a mutual NDA before the first call, and we're happy to work with clients anywhere in the world, adjusting to your time zone for calls and updates.

Is ongoing maintenance available?

Our maintenance plan is ₹999/month: updates, security patches, uptime monitoring, up to 2 hours of content edits, and priority support. Cancel anytime.
