Pricing & Packages

What's included in Starter (₹7,999)?
Up to 3 pages, responsive design, basic SEO, contact form, social media integration, 1 revision, 7 days support, delivery in 3–5 days. Perfect for portfolios, landing pages and small businesses.

Why is Business "Most Popular"?
It covers what 80% of growing businesses need — premium UI/UX, advanced SEO, WhatsApp & Google Maps integration, blog, gallery and speed optimization.

Can I upgrade later?
Yes. Upgrade within 30 days of Starter delivery and we credit the full Starter fee — you only pay the difference.

Hidden charges?
Zero. The price we quote is the price you pay. Add-ons are only added if you explicitly request them.

Custom quote?
E-commerce, booking systems, LMS, AI integration, CRM, admin panels or SaaS platforms get a tailored proposal — free consultation, reply within 24 hours.

Project Timeline & Process

How fast do you deliver?
Starter ships in 3–5 days, Business in 5–7 days, Premium in 7–10 days. We run discovery, design and development in parallel with a dedicated senior designer and engineer — no juniors, no hand-offs.

What if I don't like the design?
Every package includes revisions (up to unlimited minor revisions on Premium), plus a design sign-off milestone before development — we never build what you don't love.

How do I get started?
Book a free consultation. We'll audit your current site and share a fixed-price proposal within 48 hours.

Hosting, Domains & Ownership

Do you handle hosting and domain setup?
Yes — hosting setup from ₹1,000, domain setup from ₹500, and business email from ₹500/account. We configure everything and hand over full access.

Who owns the website?
You do — 100%. Code, design files, domain, hosting and content are all transferred to you at delivery. No lock-in, ever.

Can I edit the site myself?
Premium sites ship with CMS integration so your team can update copy, images and pages without touching code.

Can you migrate my old site?
Yes — from WordPress, Wix, Squarespace or Webflow, with 301 redirects to protect your SEO rankings.

Maintenance & Support

What support is included?
Starter includes 7 days, Business 15 days, and Premium 30 days of post-launch support — bug fixes, tweaks and guidance included.

What about ongoing maintenance?
Our maintenance plan is ₹999/month: updates, security patches, uptime monitoring, content edits (up to 2 hours) and priority support. Cancel anytime.

What if something breaks after launch?
If it's a defect in our work, we fix it free — regardless of when you find it.

SEO & Performance

Is SEO included?
Every package includes SEO — basic on Starter, advanced setup on Business, and premium technical SEO (schema, sitemaps, Core Web Vitals) on Premium. Ongoing SEO is available from ₹2,000 as an add-on.

Will my site be fast?
Speed is a feature. Business and Premium include dedicated speed optimization, and we target 95+ Lighthouse scores on every build.

Will it work on mobile?
Mobile-first is our default. We design for mobile before desktop and test across real devices before launch.

Payments

How do payments work?
50% to kick off, 50% on delivery. Larger custom projects can be split across 3 milestones. We accept UPI, bank transfer, and all major cards.

EMI or payment plans?
Available for Premium and custom projects through partner providers.

Payment gateway for my site?
We integrate Razorpay, Stripe and more from ₹2,500 as an add-on.

Trust & Guarantees

What if I'm not happy with the result?
Our guarantee is simple: if we miss the promised quality bar, performance score or ship date — we keep working for free until we hit it. Zero exceptions.

Do you sign NDAs?
Of course. We'll sign a mutual NDA before the first call. Contracts and terms are shared upfront — no hidden fees, no scope-creep clauses.

Do you work with clients outside India?
Yes — we're happy to work with clients anywhere in the world and can adjust to your time zone for calls and updates.
